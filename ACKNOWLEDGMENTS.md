## Intellectual Foundation

The system implements the **7 Core Qualities** identified by the research:

1. **Technique** (1.0x weight)
2. **Intensity/Hype** (1.2x weight)
3. **Originality/Creativity** (1.1x weight)
4. **Consistency/Foundation** (1.0x weight)
5. **Impact/Performance** (1.3x weight) ← Highest priority
6. **Musicality** (1.0x weight)
7. **Battle Intelligence** (1.1x weight)

These weights reflect the consensus from Krump masters on what makes a winning performance.

## Cultural Respect Statement

This implementation aims to honor Krump's cultural origins:

- ✅ Uses authentic Krump vocabulary (jabs, stomps, buck, rowdy, bony, etc.)
- ✅ Recognizes community values (fam, blood, hype, vibe)
- ✅ Respects the dance's origins in Black Los Angeles communities
- ✅ Acknowledges the debate around origins and narratives
- ✅ Balances technical scoring with subjective authenticity
- ✅ Centers Impact as the most important criterion (per research)
